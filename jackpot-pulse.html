Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jackpot Pulse ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to being transparent about our practices.

01 Information We Collect

Jackpot Pulse does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information directly. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the App.

The App stores one piece of data locally on your device: your last selected state lottery — saved using Apple's standard on-device storage so your preference is remembered between sessions. This data never leaves your device.

02 Third-Party Services

Jackpot Pulse uses the following third-party services, each of which may collect certain data independently under their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob (Advertising)
The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy. To opt out of personalized advertising, adjust your settings at adssettings.google.com.

The Lotto API
Lottery drawing results are fetched from The Lotto API. This is a read-only request — no personal information is sent to this service. Only your device's standard network request metadata (such as IP address) may be visible to the API server, as is the case with any internet request.

03 Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit. Jackpot Pulse does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Health or financial data
  • Browsing history
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or calendars
  • Any form of biometric data

04 Children's Privacy

Jackpot Pulse is intended for users aged 17 and older in accordance with App Store guidelines for lottery-related content. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

05 Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Your locally stored state preference can be cleared at any time by deleting the App from your device.

06 Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we take reasonable technical precautions to ensure the App operates securely, including using encrypted HTTPS connections for all network requests.

07 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

08 Contact Us
